How it works

Getting help at home
should be this simple.

01
🗣️

Tell us what's wrong

Just type what's happening — "my AC stopped cooling" or "there's a pipe leaking under my sink." We figure out what kind of help you need and find pros near you.

02
📊

See who's actually reliable

Every pro shows their real track record — how often they show up on time, how many complaints they've had, what other homeowners paid. No paid rankings. No fake stars.

03
🛡️

We look out for you

Once you've booked, TrustFix quietly keeps watch. If your pro is late, the quote looks off, or something doesn't add up — we tell you and handle it.

Your protection, built in

We watch over
your job for you.

After you book, TrustFix keeps a quiet eye on things — your contractor's arrival, the quote you were given, their recent record. If something looks off, you'll know before it costs you money.

⏰

Late contractor alert

If your contractor is more than 15 minutes late, you get a notification right away — along with a backup option already lined up. No waiting, no phone calls.

🔄

Backup already lined up

If 45 minutes pass with no show, we've already found the next best available pro nearby — same type of work, strong track record. One tap and they're coming.

💰

Overpriced quote warning

When a quote comes in significantly higher than what other homeowners typically pay for the same job, we flag it before you say yes. No surprises on your bill.

🚫

Repeat problems — removed

Contractors with repeated complaints don't just get buried in the results — they're removed entirely. The platform enforces its own standards so you don't have to.
