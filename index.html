The Autonomy Layer

An agent that works
after you book.

No other home services platform monitors your job in real-time. TrustFix's autonomous agent runs a continuous evaluation loop — catching problems before they become disasters.

⏱️

ETA Watchdog

Fires at 15 minutes past due. Logs the delay, notifies you, and queues a backup contractor with matching score and trade.

🔁

Auto Re-match

At 45 minutes overdue, the agent proposes the highest-scored available backup — one tap to confirm. No phone calls, no waiting on hold.

⚠️

Price Anomaly Detection

Quotes more than 30% above category average trigger an automatic flag. You see it before approving any work.

🚫

Contractor Suspension

Two disputes in 30 days triggers automatic suspension — no manual review needed. The platform enforces its own standards.
